Hardware Diagnostics

Thorough testing and troubleshooting to identify failed, unstable, overheating, or poorly performing computer hardware.

Request Service

What This Service Includes

Initial Inspection

The computer is inspected for visible damage, loose connections, unusual sounds, overheating signs, and other hardware concerns.

Component Testing

Major components are checked for errors, instability, failure symptoms, and proper operation.

Storage Drive Testing

Hard drives and SSDs are checked for health warnings, read errors, performance problems, and possible failure.

Memory Testing

Installed RAM is tested for errors, stability, compatibility, and proper detection.

Temperature Review

Processor, graphics, and system temperatures are checked for overheating or cooling problems.

Diagnostic Findings

You receive a clear explanation of the problem, recommended repair options, and estimated costs.

Common Problems We Can Help With

Computer Will Not Power On

The computer shows no lights, fans, display, or other signs of normal startup.

Random Shutdowns

The computer shuts off, restarts, or loses power unexpectedly during normal use.

Overheating

The computer becomes unusually hot, runs loudly, slows down, or shuts off under load.

Blue Screens and Crashes

Windows regularly crashes, freezes, displays blue screens, or reports hardware-related errors.

Unusual Noises

Clicking, grinding, buzzing, rattling, or unusually loud fan noises are coming from the computer.

Hardware Not Detected

Storage drives, RAM, graphics, USB devices, or other components are missing or not working correctly.
