Printer & Device Support

Setup and troubleshooting for printers, scanners, monitors, webcams, keyboards, mice, and other supported computer accessories.

Request Service

What This Service Includes

Compatibility Check

The computer, Windows version, device model, available ports, and required software are reviewed.

Physical Connection

The device is connected using the supported USB, display, Bluetooth, wireless, or Ethernet connection.

Driver Installation

Official drivers and required manufacturer software are installed when needed.

Device Configuration

Supported device settings, defaults, permissions, and preferences are configured.

Basic Troubleshooting

Connection problems, missing drivers, incorrect settings, and common device errors are checked.

Final Testing

The device is tested for connection, recognition, and basic operation before service is completed.

Printers

Common Problems We Can Help With

Device Is Not Detected

Windows does not recognize the connected printer, scanner, display, webcam, or accessory.

Missing or Incorrect Driver

The device appears with an error, limited features, or incorrect operation because of a driver problem.

Printer Shows Offline

The printer is listed as unavailable, paused, disconnected, or unable to receive print jobs.

Monitor Has No Picture

A connected display shows no signal, the wrong resolution, or an incorrect screen arrangement.

Camera or Microphone Not Working

A meeting or recording application cannot detect or access the correct camera or microphone.

Bluetooth Device Will Not Connect

A wireless keyboard, mouse, headset, or other accessory will not pair or remain connected.
