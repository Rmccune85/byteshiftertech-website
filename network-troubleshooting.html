Network Troubleshooting

Diagnosis and troubleshooting for slow internet, weak Wi-Fi, dropped connections, network outages, and devices that will not connect.

Request Service

What This Service Includes

Initial Network Diagnosis

The modem, router, Wi-Fi signal, connected devices, cabling, and reported symptoms are reviewed.

Speed Testing

Internet and local network speeds are tested to identify slow or inconsistent performance.

Wi-Fi Signal Testing

Wireless signal strength, interference, coverage, and connection quality are checked.

Router Review

Router settings, firmware, wireless bands, security, and device connections are reviewed.

Cable and Port Testing

Ethernet cables, router ports, wall connections, and supported wired devices are checked.

Findings and Recommendations

The cause of the problem, available repair options, and any recommended equipment changes are explained.

Common Problems We Can Help With

Slow Internet

Web pages, downloads, streaming, gaming, or video calls perform slower than expected.

Dropped Connections

Computers, phones, televisions, or other devices repeatedly lose their network connection.

Weak Wi-Fi Signal

Certain rooms or areas have poor signal, low speeds, or unreliable wireless coverage.

No Internet Connection

The modem or router appears powered on, but connected devices cannot reach the internet.

One Device Will Not Connect

A specific computer, printer, television, phone, or smart device cannot join the network.

Intermittent Network Problems

The network works sometimes but becomes slow, unstable, or unavailable without warning.
