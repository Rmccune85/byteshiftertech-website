Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need an appointment?

Yes. Please call, text, or email before scheduling a drop-off, pickup, remote session, or on-site visit.

Do you offer remote support?

Yes. Many Windows, software, email, Microsoft 365, printer, and account-related issues can be handled remotely.

Do you provide on-site service?

Select on-site services are available throughout Trumbull County and nearby communities. Travel charges may apply depending on location.

Do you work on Apple computers?

Current services primarily support Windows computers, home networks, printers, and other compatible technology.

How long will service take?

Completion time depends on the service, device condition, data size, required updates, and parts availability. An estimated timeframe is provided after the request is reviewed.

Will my files be protected?

Reasonable care is taken when handling accessible files and storage devices. Customers are encouraged to maintain a current backup because data preservation cannot be guaranteed when a drive is damaged or failing.

Will I know the cost before work begins?

Yes. A starting price or estimate is provided before service begins. Additional work, parts, or charges require your approval.

What payment methods do you accept?

Cash, debit cards, major credit cards, and approved electronic payments are accepted. Credit card payments may include a processing surcharge equal to the cost of processing, not to exceed 4%. The surcharge does not apply to cash or debit card payments.
