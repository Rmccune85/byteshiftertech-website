Common Problems We Can Help With

Software Problems

A supported program will not open, update, install, activate, or work correctly.

Email and Account Problems

Email will not send or receive, accounts will not synchronize, or sign-in help is needed.

Windows Settings

Help is needed with display, sound, notifications, startup programs, defaults, or other Windows settings.

Printer Software Problems

A connected printer shows offline, has a stuck queue, or needs driver assistance.

Browser Problems

The browser has unwanted extensions, changed settings, pop-ups, or account-sync problems.

General Computer Help

The customer needs help completing a task, finding a setting, or understanding a computer feature.

Problems That Require In-Person Service

Computer Will Not Start

Remote support cannot begin when the computer will not power on or load Windows.

No Internet Connection

The computer must have a working internet connection before remote support can be used.

Hardware Repairs

Internal components, damaged ports, overheating, and physical repairs require in-person service.

Severely Infected Computers

Serious malware or security concerns may require the computer to be inspected in person.

Network Equipment Problems

Modem, router, cabling, or Wi-Fi coverage problems may require on-site testing.

Data Recovery

Failed drives, inaccessible files, and hardware-related recovery problems cannot be completed remotely.
